DETAILS

Shimla is the capital city of the northern Indian state of Himachal Pradesh.
It's located on a ridge in the Himalayan foothills, northeast of Chandigarh. Shimla is India's largest and best known hill station.
Location State: Himachal Pradesh
Elevation: 7,100 feet (2,200 meters)
Location: Northeast of Chandigarh
History Shimla was formerly known as Simla It was the summer capital during British Rule The Kingdom of Nepal annexed Shimla
Attractions Mall road: A major tourist attraction and retail center Snowfall: The best season to visit for snow is winter, between November and February
