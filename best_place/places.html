BEST TOURIST PLACES IN INDIA

  • The Himalayas
    • From the Northern hills of Jammu and Kashmir to the North-Eastern Hills of Arunachal Pradesh, the north-eastern border of India shares The Himalayas with China. A few famous tourist spots of my choice :
      1. Hills of Kashmir; J&K. click for more details
      2. Darjeeling; West Bengal.
      3. NorthEast Hills; Arunachal Pradesh.
      4. Nainital; Uttarakhand.
      5. Shimla; UP.
      6. Shillong; Meghalaya.
  • The Great Desert
    • India has it's mystique desert in the state of Rajasthan and a part in Gujarat; with a touch of Medieval History, still preserved in a royal manner.
    • The Grand Sea-Shore
    • The Southern part of Indian Peninsula; has a few million sea-shores of tourist interest with Bay of Bengal in it's East and Arabian Sea to the west.Four famous tourist-spot are:
      • Puri
      • Chennai
      • Goa
      • Beaches of Kerala and Karnataka
  • The Wild Indian Forests
    • The Sundarbans is a natural region comprising southern Bangladesh and a small part in Eastern India. It is the largest single block of tidal halophytic mangrove forest in the world. It's in the Southern Part of West Bengal and famous for Royal Bengal Tiger.
    • In the state of Madhya Pradesh, there is a vast extent of tropical rain-forest which is mainly famous for white tigers.
    • The forest of Geer, Gujarat is the only resource for lion in India.
