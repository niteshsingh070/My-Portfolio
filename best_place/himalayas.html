  1. Hills of Kashmir; J&K.

  2. Darjeeling; West Bengal.

  3. NorthEast Hills; Arunachal Pradesh.

  4. Nainital; Uttarakhand.

  5. Shimla; UP.

  6. Shillong; Meghalaya.
